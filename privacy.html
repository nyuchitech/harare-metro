Privacy Policy

At Harare Metro, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and protect your information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding your data when you use our news aggregation service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Currently, Harare Metro does not require user registration or personal information to access our news content. We may collect information you voluntarily provide when:

  • Contacting us via email
  • Reporting technical issues
  • Providing feedback about our service

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:

  • Technical Information: Browser type, device type, operating system, IP address
  • Usage Information: Pages visited, time spent on site, referral source
  • Preferences: Theme settings (light/dark mode) stored locally in your browser

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our news aggregation service
  • Analyze website performance and user experience
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Ensure technical functionality and security
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 Third-Party Services

Harare Metro uses the following third-party services:

  • Cloudflare: For content delivery, performance optimization, and security
  • RSS Feed Providers: We aggregate publicly available news content from Zimbabwe news sources

3.2 Social Media Sharing

When you use our social sharing features (WhatsApp, Twitter, Facebook), you will be redirected to those platforms, which have their own privacy policies.

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect our rights and safety.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Data Storage

Your data is processed and stored using Cloudflare's global network, with servers located worldwide to ensure fast access to our service.

4.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Data minimization practices

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Browser Settings

You can control certain data collection through your browser settings:

  • Disable JavaScript (may affect site functionality)
  • Clear local storage and cookies
  • Use private/incognito browsing mode

5.2 Theme Preferences

Your theme preference (light/dark mode) is stored locally in your browser and can be cleared by deleting your browser data.

6. Children's Privacy

Harare Metro does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

7. International Data Transfers

As we use Cloudflare's global network, your information may be processed in countries outside Zimbabwe. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Notify users of significant changes through our website
  • Continue to protect your information in accordance with this policy
